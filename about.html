About Gene

I was born and raised in suburban Chicago, and have travelled far and wide before deciding to settle in San Diego. I have held upwards of 30 diverse jobs before turning 25, which also helped me greatly. My travels have given me experience that I can draw upon in life and art.

I am an avid game player, particularly of the Fantasy genre. This exposure has also prepared me well for the art world.

I also enjoy being outdoors, especially in or around water. Me also like surfing!
